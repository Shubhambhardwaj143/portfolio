Short Bio

I'm Shubham Bharadwaj, a web developer from near Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. I build websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and WordPress, and I focus on creating clean, practical designs that work well for clients

Alongside development, I enjoy teaching students, helping them learn how to create websites and guiding them through digital skills that can open up opportunities. For me, web development isn't just a career, it's a way to empower people to create and share their ideas online.

I believe that learning never stops, so I continuously explore new technologies, tools, and industry trends to improve my skills. My aim is to deliver high-quality work, build long-term relationships with clients and students, and make web development simple and accessible for everyone.
